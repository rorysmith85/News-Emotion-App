How does this work?

This app displays the emotion and sentiment in news headlines.

You can pick your news subject and your news sources as you like. This allows you to compare the sentiment and emotion of different news sources in order to see if there are any signicant differences on how the news is reported.

Given that most people get their news from reading headlines, understanding how they are geared to pander to our sentiment and emotinos is important in revealing bias. Can you see a trend based on subject areas? Is there a difference in scores based on whether the headlines are right-wing, centrist, or left-wing?

To understand how the data is processed check out the About section in the menu.

To start pick a news topic and then a source and hit submit.
